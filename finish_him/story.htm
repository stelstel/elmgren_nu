The Story Behind Finish Him

The swedish band Finish Him formed back in 1986 by Stefan Elmgren and Stefan Soffiantini. They met because they hung around in the same crowd based around a synthpopclub in central Stockholm.
Three days after the band formed they played their first gig as support for two other bands, New Connection (with Soffiantini as member) and Alderaan (with Elmgren).
Since then they've been composing slowly but surely.
For a time the band also contained singer Mikael Björndahl who later became a Mormon never to be heard from again.
At the moment Finish Him are more idle than ever,and no one knows if or when they'll release another classic electronic hit.
