Flickan I Mitt Badkar

    
The Girl In My Bathtub, Review Läs originalrecensionen på svenska

A goldmedal to the band Finish Him from Stockholm for this wonderful single. It contains two wholly synthetic songs without sampling (in any case it doesn't appear to be any)."Flickan i mitt badkar" is a quite speedy thing with totally incomprehensible lyrics in Swedish. Very good sound and and well produced. Simply a golden song. If you should listen to the b-side, you'd find a instrumental equally sythetic thing, called "Ensam 89". A calm song without drums. Very good! If you haven't got this ,buy it! It is, in my opinion, a "buy or die"


Andreas Ehlin
